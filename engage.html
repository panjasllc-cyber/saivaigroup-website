Direct Advisory Sessions

Focused, high-intensity strategy sessions with Anthony Russell — conducted onsite at your organization or virtually. Designed for leadership teams facing a specific strategic decision, operational challenge, or transformation initiative that requires expert facilitation and structured thinking.

Tier 3 · Retained Advisory

Retained Advisory Relationship

An ongoing strategic advisory relationship for organizations navigating sustained complexity — whether a multi-year M&A process, a post-acquisition integration, an AI transformation, or a period of significant organizational change.

Retained clients receive priority access, regular strategy sessions, and the continuity of an advisor who understands your organization's history, culture, and strategic context deeply.

  • Regular strategy sessions (cadence structured to your needs)
  • Priority access between sessions for emerging issues
  • Board and leadership team advisory support
  • M&A process advisory from evaluation through integration
  • Equity-based engagements available for qualifying situations

Retained engagements are structured individually. Investment and scope determined through discovery.
